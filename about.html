Trevor Ladner

Hello! My name is Trevor Ladner, I am a recent graduate of Purdue University with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering with Honors Distinction. I also earned minors in Intellectual Property Law, Political Science, and Engineering and Public Policy. Currently, I am a Supplier Development Engineer at SpaceX working on Starlink consumer product and Starlink aviaiton owning cables and harnessing. I previously worked at Rivian Automotive as a Interior Materials Engineer leading soft and rolled goods.

I previously had internships at Rivian and Magna International working on various product development roles including validation, part design, sourcing, DFMEA, and prototyping. While at Purdue, I was heaving involved with the Honors College Racing Crew - a team that competes in the annual Purdue Grand Prix. On the team, I served in various roles such as Crew Chief, Assistant Director, and PR/Sponsorship Lead. In these roles, I performed tasks from budget management to overseeing the engineering and racing strategy for one of our go karts. I also served as Vice President of the Purdue Mechanical Engineering Ambassadors in which I oversaw each of the committees in the organization.

Apart from this I was a member in Purdue Bands as well as a member of Tau Beta Sigma, the honorary band sorority. I was also the Lead TA for ENGR 16100/200 on campus. Outside of all this, I love rooting for Purdue Athletics, spending time with friends, watching any form of racing, baking, hiking, kayaking, and watching movies.

Feel free to reach out to me!
